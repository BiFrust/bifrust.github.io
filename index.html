This guide will help you learn how to manage your data easily, whether it’s stored in the cloud or on your local devices. Bifrust gives you a clear view of your storage space and data use. You can also move your data smoothly between different storage locations.

With our platform, you can connect and manage different storage systems without hassle. Find your files faster with our advanced search tools, whether they’re on your computer or in the cloud. Plus, you can see detailed reports to understand how your storage is being used and plan for future data moves.

If you need to move your data to a new place, our platform makes it simple to transfer files, folders, and users between storage systems and applications.
